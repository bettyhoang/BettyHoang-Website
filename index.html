Hi! I’m Betty Hoang, an aspiring UX + Visual designer. I’ve always loved to be creative through art and design, and now I am currently the UX/UI Lead at UC Riverside’s first ever Designathon and the VP of Marketing for Design@UCR, UC Riverside’s only student organization supporting students interested in UI/UX, web design, and design thinking!

In addition to my involvement in my own school extracurriculars, I also like to be active in designations and hackathons hosted by other organizations and school, winning Best UX/UI at Cutie Hack 2022 and 3rd Place Prize at Jumpstart Designathon 2023.
